Our Story

Meet the roaster

It started with curiosity, a bag of green beans, and a whole lot of wondering "what if?"

From kitchen experiments to your morning cup

Hi, I'm Zander! I'm 12 years old and I'm kind of obsessed with coffee. The smell of fresh beans, the science behind the perfect roast, the way one good cup can totally make someone's morning — I love all of it.

Tin Tie Coffee Roasters is my way of sharing that love. Every single bag is roasted by hand in small batches, so you know it's crazy fresh. I pick the beans, dial in the roast, and seal every bag myself.

The name? It comes from those little tin ties that keep coffee bags sealed tight. Simple, reliable, and gets the job done — just like my coffee.

— Zander
Fun fact!

Zander has roasted over 100 batches of coffee and can tell you the difference between beans from three continents — just by smell.

How It Works

From our roaster to your door

Three simple steps to fresh coffee magic.

🌿
1

Sourced from small farms

We order green coffee directly from small, sustainable farms around the world — because great coffee starts long before the roaster.

🔥
2

Roasted in the driveway

Small batches, big flavor. We roast right in the driveway on a homemade BBQ drum roaster my dad built from scratch. It's loud, it smells amazing, and it works perfectly.

🆕
3

Hand-stamped & Tin Tied

Every bag gets hand-stamped and sealed in our world-famous Tin Tie bags. Fresh, personal, and ready for you.
